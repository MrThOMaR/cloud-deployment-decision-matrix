# Detailed explanations of criteria

## Compliance & data protection

This criterion assesses the extent to which the respective option meets regulatory requirements. Laws such as the GDPR, industry-specific regulations (e.g., BAFIN for financial service providers, KRITIS for critical infrastructures), and internal compliance guidelines play a central role here.

## Costs (TCO 3-5 years)

The total cost of ownership includes all direct and indirect costs over a period of 3-5 years.

## Scalability

Scalability describes the ability to quickly adapt resources to changing requirements.

## Flexibility

Flexibility assesses how easily you can switch technologies, integrate new services, or make architectural changes.

## Performance & Latency

Performance includes computing power, storage speed, network throughput, and especially latency.

## Availability & SLA

This criterion evaluates the guaranteed availability of the infrastructure, typically expressed as a percentage of uptime per year.

## Network Dependency

This criterion assesses the dependence on a functioning network connection and the impact of an Internet outage on the availability of the application. You should also consider bandwidth requirements for large amounts of data and the costs of redundant internet connections.


## Security & Control

Security and control evaluate both technical security measures and organizational control over infrastructure and data.

## Time-to-Market

Time-to-market measures how quickly new applications or features can go live.


## Internal skills requirements

This criterion assesses the level of requirements for internal personnel, specialist expertise, and team size. You should also consider the availability of the necessary skills on the job market.

## Vendor lock-in risk

Vendor lock-in describes dependence on a specific provider and the difficulty of switching to another.

## Disaster Recovery

Disaster recovery assesses the ability to recover quickly after a failure.

## Innovation & Services

This criterion evaluates access to new technologies and innovative services.

## Architectural suitability

Architectural suitability assesses how well the technical architecture of the application fits the respective infrastructure option.
